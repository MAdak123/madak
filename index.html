About SPI Skill Up

Our Mission

SPI Skill Up is dedicated to making quality education accessible to everyone in Haripal and surrounding areas. We believe that learning should be free, flexible, and focused on building practical skills that lead to real career opportunities.

What We Offer

We curate the best free online courses from top universities and industry leaders around the world. Our platform helps you discover courses that match your interests and career goals, with detailed information about what you'll learn, course duration, and potential job opportunities.

Local Focus

Based in Haripal, Hooghly, West Bengal, we understand the specific needs of our community. We provide guidance on local job opportunities and help bridge the gap between education and employment in our region.
